# Android APK Creation Guide

## Quick Options:

### 1. PWA (Recommended - No APK needed)
Your app is already a PWA! Users can:
1. Open your Netlify site on Android Chrome
2. Tap menu (3 dots) → "Add to Home screen"
3. Install like a native app

### 2. Online APK Builders
- PWA Builder: https://www.pwabuilder.com/
- Convert your web app to APK in minutes

### 3. WebView App (Basic)
If you want a simple APK that loads your site:

Required files:
- Android Studio
- Basic Android project
- WebView component loading your Netlify URL
